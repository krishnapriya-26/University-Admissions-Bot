Overview

The University Admissions Bot is a web-based chatbot designed to assist students with university admission queries. It provides instant responses related to courses, eligibility, deadlines, and application processes.



Problem Statement

Students often struggle to find clear and structured admission information. This bot simplifies the process by providing automated responses in a conversational interface.
